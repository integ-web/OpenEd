# Capstone Stages + Deliverables V4

## Stage 0 — Briefing room
Inputs: Aster-3 system card, deployment proposal, known safeguards, known limitations.
Output: begin dossier review.
Gate: none.

## Stage 1 — System and release context
Learner completes:
- System charter
- Intended users
- Tool access
- Data access
- Deployment surface
- Release candidates
- Decision owner
Gate: at least two deployment surfaces and one decision owner.

## Stage 2 — Threat model assembly
Learner imports or creates:
- Threat actor
- Harm pathway
- Capability hypothesis
- Access condition
- Safeguard assumptions
- Severe harm endpoint
Gate: at least three risk domains; at least one benign-use failure mode and one adversarial-use failure mode.

## Stage 3 — Evaluation portfolio design
Required six evaluations:
1. Capability evaluation
2. Safety/red-team evaluation
3. Agent/tool-use evaluation
4. Multimodal/spatial evaluation
5. Production/telemetry evaluation
6. Governance/reporting evaluation
Each must name claim, task family, harness, dataset/source, metric, baseline, validity threats, and decision relevance.

## Stage 4 — Evidence room
Learner creates 12 evidence cards from fictional packets.
Gate: 12 cards; at least 4 weaken release case; at least 3 need replication; at least 1 benchmark validity concern.

## Stage 5 — Risk dashboard
Learner assigns risk levels across domains.
Gate: no risk level without evidence; no acceptable risk without mitigation rationale.

## Stage 6 — Threshold memo
Fields:
- Decision requested
- Threshold level implicated
- Evidence for release
- Evidence against release
- Remaining uncertainty
- Required mitigations
- Monitoring requirements
- Recommendation
- Confidence
Gate: residual uncertainty note and conditions for reversing decision.

## Stage 7 — Executive report
Fields:
- What changed
- What we tested
- What we found
- What remains uncertain
- What it means for release
- Recommendation
- Evidence appendix
Gate: cite at least six evidence cards and two limitations.

## Stage 8 — Defense simulation
Reviewer challenges:
1. Product leader: Why not ship faster?
2. Safety lead: Which finding worries you most?
3. External evaluator: Which result would you not trust yet?
Gate: pass two of three challenges.

## Stage 9 — Portfolio export
Output:
- Portfolio-ready case study
- Evidence appendix
- Artifact list
- Final recommendation
- What I would do next
Gate: all prior gates complete.
